Incident: Failed login attempts detected from endpoint.

1. Why did the alert trigger?
Multiple failed logins occurred.

2. Why were multiple failed logins happening?
Incorrect credentials were repeatedly entered.

3. Why was account protection weak?
No strict lockout policy was enforced.

4. Why was detection needed?
Unauthorized access attempts can lead to compromise.

5. Why improve controls?
To reduce brute-force risk and strengthen security posture.
